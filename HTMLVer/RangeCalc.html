How to use

This app is designed to allow you to calculate a range to a target using a compass. All you have to do is select two fixed points. Go to the first fixed point and take a bearing to the target. Then go to the second fixed point and take a second bearing to the target. Then measure the distance between those two fixed points. The calculated range is the distance from between the two fixed points to the target.
